BEST SIP IN THE TOWN

FRESH ROOHAFZA

take a bottle of Rooh Afza syrup. Place 225ml (7.6oz) of water or milk in a 350ml (12oz) glass. Add 2 tbsp (40ml) of Rooh Afza syrup to water or milk (more or less according to taste). Stir until the syrup is completely dissolved. Add 1 or 2 teaspoons of sugar, if desired, to taste. Add two or three mint leaves and ice cubes. You can also add some chopped pistachio nuts or slivered almonds to your drink.
